CMAKE_MACOSX_RPATH¶

Whether to use rpaths on macOS and iOS.

This variable is used to initialize the MACOSX_RPATH property on all targets.

이전 항목

CMAKE_MACOSX_BUNDLE

다음 항목

CMAKE_MAP_IMPORTED_CONFIG_<CONFIG>
