Green Hills MULTI¶

Generates Green Hills MULTI project files (experimental, work-in-progress).

Customizations are available through the following cache variables:

  • GHS_BSP_NAME

  • GHS_CUSTOMIZATION

  • GHS_GPJ_MACROS

  • GHS_OS_DIR

備註

This generator is deemed experimental as of CMake 3.7.2 and is still a work in progress. Future versions of CMake may make breaking changes as the generator matures.
